Indian weddings! There is no doubt that in Indian culture, weddings are a big fat event. People spend most of their life savings in throwing lavish parties with the most scrumptious food. Tons of food is prepared for the grand wedding celebrations. Well who doesn’t enjoy all the delicious stuff but the question that arises is all the food actually consumed? The answer to this is NO. There’s a lot of leftover food after the event usually and where does all of this goes?

The leftover food at a wedding is a very common issue, but the main challenge that comes is to not let that food get wasted. According to several types of research, approximately 40 percent of the food prepared at a wedding venue is wasted and thrown away after the wedding ends. India’s estimated 10 million weddings a year contribute to food wastage. The bottom line is we cannot ignore the growing concern regarding food wastage in Indian weddings. If your conscience doesn’t allow you to let this food go waste, here’s our website to help you with the same.
